About the Exhibition

Behind every shadow, there is light that guides the story.I am a photographer who sees the world through the contrast between light and dark. For me, shadows are not merely the absence of light, but a space for emotions and meanings that are often hidden. Through this work, I strive to uncover the quieter aspects of life: fleeting moments, brief glances, and the silence that holds thousands of stories. Each photograph in the exhibition Through the Lens of Shadows is a captured moment meant to remind us that beauty isnt always in the spotlight—sometimes it hides in the shadows of light. Photography, for me, is an introspective journey—a way to recognize oneself, the world, and the shadows between them.
